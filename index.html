Facebook Chat Auto Deleter

Automates deletion of Facebook Messenger conversations on facebook.com/messages. Press Start to open the thread menu → choose Delete chat → confirm, with an optional delay and live counters.

  • One-click Start/Stop controls
  • Deleted / Total progress counters
  • Custom delay between deletions
  • Runs only on Messenger pages
